About Us

Who Are We?

           AquaVive is a website designed by a team of passionate individuals called UIA or Undergraduates in Action, dedicated to spread awareness on the ongoing degradation and destruction of Philippine Coral Reefs. Our established skillset in creative designing, research, and multimedia specialities came together to make a positive impact on our country's aquatic ecosystems.

What is Our Mission?

           Our mission is to inspire communities to take action in preserving marine life through education, innovative projects, and collaborative efforts. We believe in a sustainable future where the beauty and diversity of our aquatic ecosystems are protected for generations to come.
